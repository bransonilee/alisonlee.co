<font color="00B394">Passages</font> is a magazine dedicated to the modern traveller. Each issue dives into and explores a destination somewhere in the world and aims to transport its readers there. The magazine acts as an inspiration and motivation to discover what lies in the beyond.
It was designed with plenty of negative space to highlight the unchartered territories of the world. Careful consideration was given into the placement of text and imagery to create a sense of excitement and thrill. The magazine is 11 x 17 in. when printed for the images to have the most impact.
